This year my Vietnamese auntie gifted me a food package with 10 different self-cooked dishes carefully wrapped and express delivered all the way from the south of Germany yay!

Around the corner on a computer the size of a fridge, Germany's first email over the expanded ARPANET'S CSNET was received from the US and Prof Zorn further helped to set up the Beijing node thereby also receiving China's first email that read "Across the Great Wall we can reach every corner in the world".

According to the professor it was being labeled exotic science back in the 80s and deffo I was exotic studying at this wannabe mit aka karlsruhe institute of technology later.

Being a 2nd-gen refugee female of color in tech can hurt e.g. with a CS male-female ratio of 91:9 when I started but you really care about your parents, auntie, uncle & co being yelled at, made fun of, or insulted for the way we look and speak.
